About Me

I am an Information Systems student with an interest in system analysis, business technology, and user-centered design. My coursework has helped me strengthen both technical and problem-solving skills through hands-on projects.

In IS 436, I learned how to approach systems through the Software Development Life Cycle, including planning, requirements analysis, design, and implementation. I also gained experience with wireframing, UI/UX design, GitHub, database concepts, and Docker.

My goal is to continue growing as a technology professional by building systems that are practical, efficient, and easy for users to understand.
